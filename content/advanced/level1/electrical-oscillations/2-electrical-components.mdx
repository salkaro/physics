## Definitions

**Resistor ($R$)**

- Reduces the rate of flow of charge

$$
R = \dfrac{V}{i}, \quad P = i^2R
$$

**Capacitor ($C$)**

- Stores charge on parallel plates

$$
C = \dfrac{Q}{V}, \quad E_{elec} = \dfrac{1}{2} \dfrac{Q^2}{C}
$$

**Inductor ($L$)**

- Stores energy in a magnetic field

$$
L = \dfrac{V}{\dfrac{di}{dt}}, \quad E_{mag} = \dfrac{1}{2}Li^2
$$
